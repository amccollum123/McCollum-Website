MeeperBot

Recommended for Grades 2 - 5+

Construct a cool robot with LEGO on top of a motorized building platform.

MeeperBot is a programmable bot that brings your building bricks to life. With an easy-to-use programming tool that uses drag-and-drop commands, students can drive their own LEGO-like creations attached to the MeeperBot.
