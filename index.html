A Short Bio

I am an instructional technology facilitator and electives teacher at an elementary Magnet school in Raleigh. I am excited to soon be completing the Masters of Learning Design and Technology.

My Favorite Edtech Tools

  • Nearpod - I like the interactive elements in Nearpod. It makes it easy for teachers and students to share resources, answer questions, complete quizzes, investigate 3D objects, explore VR videos, and more.
  • Goosechase - This tool allows you to create fun scavenger hunts. Users complete challenges, submitting answers as texts, photos, or video. I love using it with students and staff.
  • Flipgrid - Flipgrid is a quick way for students to share thier video responses. With a very simple interface the students intuitively know how to use it. They love being able to view each others' responses as well as leave video feedback for each other.
