Dash

Recommended for Grades 2 - 5+

Using accessories, Dash can launch balls, draw pictures, collect objects, and much more.

Dash is an inviting robot that allows students to explore loops, events, conditions, and sequences by programming using Blockly code. Dash can navigate objects, dance, sing, and even respond to voice commands.
