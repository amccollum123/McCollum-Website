Sphero

Recommended for Grades 2- 5+

Design programs using pictorial, block, and/or text-based coding programs.

Sphero is a programmable robotic ball that allows students to play games, create programs, and complete challenges. Sphero allows endless opportunities for students to be creative as they complete hands-on activities.
